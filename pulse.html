WHAT IS
THE FRI?
The FORGED Resilience Index (FRI) is the proprietary diagnostic instrument of the Resilience Coaching Lab — developed by Jacob Ntintin Orange, grounded in 23 years of talent, leadership and organisational development practice across African institutions. It measures 7 distinct dimensions of how leaders respond under pressure and produces a single, comparable score for each. It is not a personality test and it does not diagnose. It reveals patterns — so you can choose, deliberately, what to strengthen next.
